Community engagement is one of our core principles. Deploying our coding camps in “pop-up” locations allows us to engage with our students and their families in the communities where they live, work and play. With technology changing the way we do nearly every job, from manual labor to banking to healthcare to law enforcement, Code Differently exists to bolster a culture of learning that is vital now and will be essential in the future.
